## Overview

This deck was preceded by a list called Twiddlestorm in Modern, where the gameplan was to untap mana-producing sources for mana, and sink that mana into a line that won the game. With the historic format getting more and more cards, this gameplan is reincarnated into a deck that untaps [[Lotus Field]], casting cantrips that untap it to build storm for a lethal [[Grapeshot]]. 

## Win Conditions

This deck builds storm until we can find a [[Grapeshot]] or [[Tendrils of Agony]] out of the sideboard.

## Pilot Notes

### Combo Preparation
The first priority is finding a [[Lotus Field]] that we can play without sacrificing itself. This means we either need to play 3 turns to have other lands to sacrifice, or we need to do something unfair like stifling the sacrifice trigger with [[Consign to Memory]] or actual [[Stifle]]. 

With the untap card quality in Historic, we have to rely on specific cards like [[Pore Over the Pages]] or [[Hidden Strings]] to go mana-positive. Pore requires that we're generating 5 mana with our untaps - which we can satisfy by playing [[Deserted Temple]] alongside our Lotus Field.

Finally, [[Underworld Breach]] or [[Past in Flames]] gives us recursion for our spells so that we can avoid fizzling.
