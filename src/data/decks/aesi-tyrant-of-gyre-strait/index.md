## Overview

Aesi, Tyrant of Gyre Strait is a legendary Simic commander from Modern Horizons 2 that rewards you for playing lands. Every land you play draws you a card, and Aesi grants you an additional land drop per turn, enabling explosive turns that flood your hand and board.

## Win Conditions

The deck pursues multiple lines to victory:

- **Scapeshift** into a pile of lands including Valakut, the Molten Pinnacle for a lethal damage burst
- **Horn of Greed** loops with Crucible of Worlds and fetch lands to draw the entire deck
- **Finale of Devastation** for a lethal alpha strike with an enormous X value

## Key Synergies

- Aesi triggers on **each** land you play — bounce lands like Simic Growth Chamber let you replay them for additional draws
- **Kodama of the East Tree** pairs with Aesi to chain free land drops
- Exploration effects stack with Aesi's built-in bonus land drop

## Pilot Notes

This deck rewards knowing your land count at all times. Prioritize establishing Aesi behind interaction rather than playing her into open mana. The deck runs enough shuffle effects to reset an over-sculpted hand.
